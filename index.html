Why the Sologne is so renowned ?

Sologne is a region in central France, characterized by its vast forests, ponds and rich and varied fauna. It is famous for its art of traditional hunting, practiced for centuries. Sologne is also renowned for its local products such as game, mushrooms, wines and cheeses. In addition to its preserved and wild nature, Sologne is rich in cultural and historical heritage, with many castles, mansions and other vestiges testifying to its prestigious past.
